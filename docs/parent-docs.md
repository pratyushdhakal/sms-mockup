# SMS (School Management System) Parent Portal

> **School:** Vidya School, Biratnagar, Morang, Nepal
> **Academic Year:** 2083

---

## 1. Dashboard

The parent dashboard provides a summary of a selected child's progress. A child selector at the top lets parents switch between their children. Three stat cards show the selected child's attendance percentage, pending fees amount, and number of upcoming exams. Quick Links provide one-click navigation to Attendance, Results, Assignments, and Fees pages.

---

## 2. Student Summary

This page gives a detailed overview of the selected child. It is organized into three panels:

**Student Details** -- Displays name, class and section, roll number, batch, phone number, and email.

**Attendance Summary** -- Shows the total count of Present, Absent, Late, and Leave records with color-coded icons (green, red, amber, blue).

**Fee Status** -- Shows the total fees, total paid (green), and outstanding balance (red if positive).

---

## 3. Child Attendance

Attendance records for the selected child. Four stat cards at the top show the total count of Present, Absent, Late, and Leave records. Below the stats, a table lists each record with date, status badge (color coded green for Present, red for Absent, amber for Late, blue for Leave), and source (manual or device). This is a read-only view.

---

## 4. Child Results

Published exam results for the selected child. Each published exam appears as a card with the exam name and date range. Inside, a table lists every subject with obtained marks, full marks, pass marks, percentage, letter grade (A+, A, B+, and so on), and pass/fail indicator (green check or red cross). A summary section shows the total marks, overall percentage, and overall grade. Only results that the admin has published are visible.

---

## 5. Child Assignments

Assignment status for the selected child. A table lists each assignment with title, teacher name, subject, due date, and a status badge: Submitted (green with check icon) or Pending (amber with clock icon). This lets parents track whether their child has completed their homework.

---

## 6. Fee History

Fee payment records for the selected child. Three stat cards show total fees, total paid (green), and outstanding balance (red if positive). Below the stats, a table lists each record with amount, paid amount, status badge (Paid, Due, or Partial with matching colors), and date.

---

## 7. Announcements

School announcements targeted at All or Parents audiences appear here. Each card shows the title, content, priority badge (High in red, Medium in amber, Low in blue), audience label, and publish date. This keeps parents informed about school events and policy updates.

---

## 8. Calendar

School events organized into two tabs: General (holidays, sports days, picnics) and Academic (exams, parent-teacher meetings, deadlines). Each event card shows the title, description, date, and type badge. Events are created by the admin and are view-only for parents.
